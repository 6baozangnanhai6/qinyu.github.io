System Overview

Figure 1. An overview of the Seed-TTS inference pipeline. (1) The speech tokenizer learns tokens from reference speech. (2) The autoregressive language model generates speech tokens based on text and reference speech. (3) The diffusion transformer generates continuous speech representations from tokens (coarse-to-fine). (4) The acoustic vocoder outputs high-quality speech.
